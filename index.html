Welcome to Faith Academy, Canaanland Directory

Faith Academy is a Christian Secondary School located in Canaanland, Ota which is in the outskirts of Lagos, Nigeria border between Lagos and Ogun state. Faith Academy was established in 1999 by the Winners' Chapel Church and presently has nearly 2,000 students and all reside in the school premises as boarders.

Vision and Mission Statement

Our Vision

To be a network of schools committed to serve as a Springboard to drive Excellence, Spiritual prowess and creativity on the platform of secondary education as insired by sound biblical principles and purposeful living with the aim of producing high level instructions.

Our Mission

Faith academy schools are co-education Chrisitian Mission Secondary Schools establised to provide high level professional instructions and learning that will cluminate in Academic Excellence, Spiritual Prowess and Creativity in her Students.
